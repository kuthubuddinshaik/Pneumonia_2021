Pneumonia - What it is?

Pneumonia is an inflammatory condition of the lung primarily affecting the small air sacs known as alveoli. Symptoms typically include some combination of productive or dry cough, chest pain, fever and difficulty breathing .The severity of the condition is variable.
Pneumonia are majorly caused by infections of bacteria or viruses and sometimes by other microorganisms.
Chest X-Ray and blood tests are commonly used to confirm the diagnosis. If pneumonia is severe, the affected person is hospitalized with oxygen support.
